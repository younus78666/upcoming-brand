### SECTION 15 — FAQ

**Eyebrow:** Common questions

**H2:** WordPress web design questions Maryland business owners ask before hiring us

**FAQ Pairs:**

**Q: How much does a WordPress website cost in Maryland?**
A: Most Maryland small business WordPress websites fall between $1,500 and $10,000 depending on page count, custom features, and the level of design work involved. A straightforward 8-page service site for a contractor or solo practitioner starts around $1,500–$3,500. A 15–20 page site with custom design, SEO setup, and blog integration runs $3,500–$6,000. Complex builds with WooCommerce, appointment booking, or IDX integration start at $6,000. Every quote we send is fixed-scope — one price, no hourly surprises.

**Q: How long does it take to build a WordPress website?**
A: Starter sites (up to 8 pages) take 4–6 weeks from signed scope to live launch. Growth sites (up to 20 pages with custom design) take 6–8 weeks. Custom builds take 8–10 weeks. Timeline is heavily influenced by how quickly you can provide feedback — our design review and revision rounds are where most delays happen. We build in dedicated review days for each phase so the process moves predictably.

**Q: Will my site score 90+ on Google PageSpeed?**
A: Yes — we commit to it in writing. Every site we launch targets a PageSpeed score of 90 or above on both mobile and desktop, with LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) under 2.5 seconds, INP (Interaction to Next Paint) under 200ms, and all three Core Web Vitals passing in Google Search Console. For context: only 44% of WordPress sites on mobile pass all three Core Web Vitals. We're aiming for the other 56% to be your competition.

**Q: Do you work with law firms, dental practices, and other regulated industries?**
A: Yes. Law firm, dental practice, and medical clinic websites make up a significant portion of our Maryland client base. We understand the compliance considerations (HIPAA-aware forms, attorney ethics rules for testimonials), the conversion patterns (booking vs. inquiry vs. phone call), and the schema types (LegalService, Dentist, MedicalOrganization) that help these sites rank in competitive local searches.

**Q: Can you migrate my existing Wix or Squarespace site to WordPress?**
A: Yes — without losing your existing search rankings. We build a complete 301 redirect map before any DNS changes happen, run a pre-launch ranking benchmark, and check rank parity in Search Console for the 30 days after launch. Most migrations see no ranking loss and often see improvement within 60 days because the WordPress build is faster and better structured for SEO.

**Q: What's included in your monthly care plans?**
A: Care plans start at $150/month and include weekly WordPress core and plugin updates, off-site backups to S3 or Google Drive, uptime monitoring with alerting, a 4-hour SLA for support tickets, and a monthly performance report. You communicate directly with the founder — not a help-desk system. Higher-tier plans include content updates, SEO reporting, and quarterly strategy reviews.

**Q: Do you write the website content, or do I?**
A: Both options are available. We can work from your existing copy, or we can write it for you. Content writing is scoped separately — typically $150–$300 per page depending on research required. For industry-specific pages (law firm practice areas, dental service pages), we use a structured brief process so the content reflects your specific practice and location, not boilerplate copy.

**Q: Do you build outside of Maryland?**
A: Yes — about 40% of our projects are from clients outside Maryland. We're based in Maryland and have deep experience with the local market (Baltimore, Annapolis, Frederick, DMV metro), but we work with clients across the US. Remote collaboration works well; on-site meetings are available within 90 minutes of Washington DC.

**Q: What platform do you build on?**
A: All our sites are built on self-hosted WordPress.org using Elementor Pro as the page builder and Astra Pro as the parent theme. This combination gives you a site you fully own, can edit yourself without a developer, and can move to a different host at any time. We don't lock you into a proprietary platform or a hosting plan you can't leave.

**Q: What happens if I need changes after launch?**
A: Small changes (text, images, adding a page) are included free for 30 days after launch. After that, one-off changes are billed at a flat hourly rate, or you can join a care plan which includes unlimited small changes each month. We don't disappear after launch — that's one of the most common complaints we hear about other Maryland web agencies.
